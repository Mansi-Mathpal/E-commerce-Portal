List Products

What is a listing? A listing refers to the process of
registering your product on the Me & Mine
platform, making it visible to customers, and
enabling them to view and purchase your
product. It involves creating a detailed product
page that includes essential information such
as product title, description, images, pricing,
and other relevant details. A well-crafted
listing helps attract potential customers and
facilitates the sale of your product on Me & Mine.

Did you know that providing precise and comprehensive information about your product, along
with clear and captivating images, can increase its visibility on our platform by up to 15%.

Storage & Shipping

Congratulations on receiving your first order! When it comes to shipping your products to
customers, Me & Mine understands the importance of fast and reliable delivery in secure
packaging.

We provide two fulfilment options for you to choose from

  Fulfilment by Me & Mine (FBM&M)

  Non Fulfilment by Me & Mine (NFBM&M)

Consider the fulfilment option that best suits your needs and preferences to ensure timely and secure delivery of your products to Me & Mine customers.

Fulfilment by Me & Mine (FBM&M)

Eliminate worries about storage, packing, shipping, and delivery by leveraging Me &
Mine's Fulfilment by Me & Mine (FBM&M) service. FBM&M offers a comprehensive
solution that handles all your shipping needs under one roof. With FBM&M, you
can entrust Me & Mine to efficiently manage the entire process, from storing your
products to expertly packing and shipping them to customers. Enjoy a hassle-free
experience and focus on growing your business while Me & Mine takes care of the logistics.

  Pick-up from seller location to Me & Mine warehouse

  FAssured badge

  Faster delivery to customer

  Seamless order processing

  Customer returns handled end to end

  Warehouse space

  Quality selection recommendation

  Rigorous quality checks

  Quality packaging materials

Non Fulfilment by Me & Mine (NFBM&M)

With Non-Fulfillment by Me & Mine (NFBM&M), you can benefit from end-to-end delivery of your products
directly from your location to the customer. In NFBM&M, it is the responsibility of the seller to ensure
that the product is properly packed and ready for dispatch before the Me & Mine agent arrives at the
seller's location. This service allows you to maintain control over the packaging process while
leveraging Me & Mine's logistics network for efficient and reliable delivery.

  Delivery to 19000+ pin codes across India

  Tracking of your product

  Customer returns support

  Logistics support from community warehouse available

FAssured by Me & Mine is an exclusive reliability program designed to offer additional visibility and benefits
to your products. By participating in FAssured, your products undergo extra quality checks and are
guaranteed to be delivered within 2-4 days of ordering. The FAssured tag not only ensures faster
delivery but also brings increased visibility, higher quality, and more orders for your products. It
serves as Me & Mine's seal of trust, emphasising reliability and speed. Earning the FAssured badge
can lead to higher revenue and garner greater trust from customers, enhancing your overall online
selling experience on Me & Mine.
